A WI-FI USB dongle also known as a WI-FI adaptapter, is a small device that connects to a computer or other device via a UBS port to provide wireless internet connectivity.It's essentially a wireless network adaptapter that alllows devices without built-in WI-FI capabilities to connect to available WI-FI networks. If your laptop, desktop, or other device doesn't have a built-in Wi-Fi card, a Wi-Fi USB dongle allows you to connect to wireless networks. It transmits and receives Wi-Fi signals, allowing your device to communicate with nearby Wi-Fi routers.
